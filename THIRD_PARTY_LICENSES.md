## PT Serif font (src/utils/ptSerifFont.js)

The embedded PT Serif Regular and Bold TTF fonts (base64-encoded, merged
Latin+Cyrillic subsets) used for formal document generation (contracts,
offers, claims) are part of the PT Serif family by ParaType, designed
specifically for Cyrillic and Latin text, commissioned by Rospechat.
Licensed under the SIL Open Font License, Version 1.1.

https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/ptserif
https://scripts.sil.org/OFL

PT Serif is © ParaType and is used here under the terms of the SIL OFL 1.1,
which permits embedding, bundling, and redistribution.
